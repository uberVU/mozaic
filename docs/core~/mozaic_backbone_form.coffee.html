    class MozaicBackboneForm extends Backbone.Form
¶

An extension of Backbone forms to support rendering of the form over a predefined template containing fields placeholder. In your template you can define a field

and when the form is rendered the field will be rendered in the data-field div. Overwrites the BBF custom behaviour of inserting all fields in it's form element to support custom templates
